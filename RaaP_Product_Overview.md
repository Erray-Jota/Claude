# RaaP - Rooms as a Product
## Product Overview for Non-Technical Audience

---

## What is RaaP?

RaaP (Rooms as a Product) is a **modular apartment building design and cost analysis platform** that helps real estate developers, architects, and construction companies plan and evaluate modular construction projects quickly and efficiently.

Think of it as a digital planning tool that lets you design an apartment building and instantly see how much it will cost, how long it will take to build, and how much money you could save by using modular construction instead of traditional building methods.

---

## Who Is This For?

- **Real Estate Developers** - Planning new apartment projects
- **Construction Companies** - Evaluating modular vs traditional construction
- **Architects & Designers** - Optimizing building layouts
- **Property Investors** - Analyzing project feasibility and returns

## Key Features

### 1. **Real-Time Cost Estimation**
As you make design choices, costs update instantly. No waiting for quotes or estimates.

### 2. **Regional Cost Factors**
The system knows that building in San Francisco costs more than building in Phoenix. It automatically applies accurate regional multipliers.

### 3. **Intelligent Unit Optimization**
You set targets (e.g., 40 studios, 40 one-beds, 30 two-beds), and the system optimizes the actual mix to fit efficiently in your building dimensions.

### 4. **Visual Design Tools**
- See your building in 3D renderings
- View floor plans with unit layouts
- Watch walkthrough videos of the construction process

### 5. **Location-Based Intelligence**
Simply type a city name or zip code, and the system:
- Finds the exact location
- Calculates regional cost factors
- Determines the nearest factory
- Adjusts all estimates accordingly

### 6. **Comparison Analysis**
See side-by-side comparisons of:
- Site-built vs Modular costs
- Different unit mix scenarios
- Various building configurations

## Real-World Example

### Scenario: Alpine Vista Apartments

**The Project:**
- 100-unit apartment building in San Francisco
- Mix of studios, 1-beds, and 2-beds
- 5 stories tall

**The Process:**
1. **Enter Location** - "San Francisco, CA"
2. **Set Requirements** - 40 studios, 30 one-beds, 30 two-beds, 5 floors
3. **Review Design** - System optimizes to fit units efficiently
4. **Analyze Costs** - Instantly see:
   - Site-built cost: $25.5M
   - Modular cost: $22.9M
   - **Savings: $2.6M** (10%+)
   - **Time saved: 9 months**

**The Decision:**
Developer can immediately see that modular construction saves over $2.5 million and gets tenants in 9 months sooner (meaning 9 months of earlier rental income). This makes the decision clear and quantifiable.
